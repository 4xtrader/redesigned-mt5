Getting Started with MT5

Follow these steps to begin trading on the latest MT5 platform:

1. Installation & Setup

  1. Download the appropriate MT5 version for your device
  2. Install following the on-screen instructions
  3. Launch MT5 and select "Open an Account"
  4. Choose your broker and complete registration

2. Navigating the Interface

Key components of the MT5 workspace:

  • Market Watch: View tradable instruments
  • Toolbox: Access news, alerts, and mail
  • Navigator: Manage accounts, EAs, and indicators
  • Terminal: Monitor trades and account history

3. Placing Your First Trade

  1. Right-click an instrument in Market Watch
  2. Select "New Order"
  3. Set trade size, stop loss, and take profit
  4. Choose execution type
  5. Click "Buy" or "Sell" to execute
